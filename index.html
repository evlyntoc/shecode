About Us

We are a non-profit organization focused on celebrating and empowering young Girls and Women in Technology across Africa.

Vision


An Africa where women are equally represented across all career roles in Technology

Mission

To build a community that embodies technical growth, networking, mentorship and visibility amongst all level career roles in Technology

Values

Team work, Community, Technical growth, Leadership, Visibility

Learn More

MEMBERS BENEFITS



COMMUNITY SUPPORT

We provide support through our diverse offline and online communities. You can trust our amazing programs and initiatives to always keep you engaged.



MONITORED GROWTH

We provide resources and monitor your growth every step of the way through regular check-ins to ensure you are hitting your personal and career goals.



GUIDANCE

Through the SCA Mentorship program and various mentors available on our platform, you are able to get help and guidance whenever you need it .



PAIR SUPPORT

Network and meet with people who have walked or are walking in the same path as you are that can relate with your experience and help you on your journey.
